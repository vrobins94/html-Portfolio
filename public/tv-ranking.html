My Top 3 Favorite Shows of All Time

The Office (2005)

The Funniest Show Ever Made.
You'll be hard pressed to find a show that can provide hilarity for 9 seasons straight.
This show is full of humorous takes, relatable office scenarios, and a cast that feels like old friends.

Sex and the City (1998)

An Iconic Show, Still Relevant Today.
Full of dating mishaps that feel all too familiar, and a cast of characters who you've probably met or embody.
This show will either have you wanting to get married or running for the hills of Singledom.

Living Single (1993)

A Show That Feels like Sitting With Family.
With Brooklyn as it's backdrop and a tight-knit group of friends, we get a glimspe into this lively time period.
This show is full of light-hearted laughter and is the perfect companion to a long day at home.
